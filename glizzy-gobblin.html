Bug Soup

This is Gobbledog's classic glizzy recipe from Berdusk's very own Void Festival. Every year people from all over the plane travel to get a bite of these tasty glizzys. Be careful though, they can be SPICY!

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds ground meat
  • Handful dried thyme.
  • 2 Fireball peppers
  • 10 wild onions
  • 4 feet of sausage casings
  • Loaf of bread

This recipe is so rewarding to prepare, if you can handle the spice! Start by grinding your meat. Add in the thyme and crushed Fireball peppers. Use this mixture to fill the sausage casings and twist every 6-9 inches. After filling and divinding out all the meat you can begin cooking. Gobbledog recommends smoking the glizzys for 12 hours. After they are done cooking, dice your onions and you are ready to go! All you have to do to prepare is stick the sausage in your bread and top with onions. Be sure to keep water nearby and enjoy this classic!
