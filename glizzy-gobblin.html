Gobbledog's Glizzys

This is Gobbledog's classic glizzy recipe from Berdusk's very own Void Festival. Every year people from all over the plane travel to get a bite of these tasty glizzys. Be careful though, they can be SPICY!

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds ground meat
  • Handful dried thyme.
  • 2 Fireball peppers
  • 10 wild onions
  • 4 feet of sausage casings
  • Loaf of bread

Steps

  1. Start by grinding your meat.
  2. Finely chop the thyme and Fireball peppers.
  3. Add herbs and spices to ground meat and mix until homogenous.
  4. Use this mixture to fill the sausage casings and twist every 6-9 inches.
  5. Smoke the glizzys for 12 hours.
  6. Just before they are done cooking dice you onion and slice your bread.
  7. Load your glizzy onto a slice of bread and top with dice onion.

Be sure to keep water nearby while you enjoy this classic!
