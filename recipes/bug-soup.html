Bug Soup

This is a classic from Tal'Dorei! I learned it from an Aarakockra in the Verdant Expanse. This tasty soup will have you SCREAMING for more! Add firebugs from the Underdark for extra spice!

Ingredients

  • 50 grubs
  • 2 hooves
  • 1 bucket of water
  • 5 wild onions
  • A pinch of Allspice

Steps

  1. Bring the water to a slow boil.
  2. Shave off outer layers of the hooves and wash.
  3. Roughly chop your onions and add to the water, along with the cleaned hooves and allspice.
  4. Allow this to boil for an hour.
  5. Take the water off the heat and add your grubs.
  6. Once the soup has cooled it is ready to be eaten.

I hope you enjoy this delicious and filling recipe! It keeps for a day, so be sure to slurp it up fast. I also highly recommend using the firebugs if you are able to get your hands on some!
