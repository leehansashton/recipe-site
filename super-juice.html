Erathale's Super Juice

Erathale is a good friend of mine and if there is one thing he knows how to do, that is cooking up one nutritious meal! This might not be the tastiest recipe out there, but you would be hard pressed to find one better suited for adventuring.

Ingredients

  • 1 gallon of fresh water
  • 3 shredded cabbages
  • 2 pounds of diced vegatables
  • 3 pounds of potatoes

Steps

  1. Bring your water up to a boil and add in all of the Ingredients.
  2. Allow the stew to simmer for a minimum of 2 hours and up to 4 hours.
  3. After the stew is done cooking, begin to mash it all together.
  4. Once you have reached a consistent texture, the dish is done!

This recipe makes 6 servings and lasts 3 days. Enjoy!
